Velo Cycling Club
'Skills, speed, confidence!'

Join the club for fun on bikes. It’s chill, friendly and supportive.
There’s no pressure to race but lots of encouragement if you’re up for it.
We’ll give you as much advice and info as we can, whatever you want to try.
Plus, plenty of shits’n’giggles to be had in our skills sessions
(although we’re serious about improving skills, speed and confidence!)

Road.

Racing for the first time?
Everything is going to be ok.

Crit.

We take part in many crit races all over the world.
Be it something famous like Red Hook Crit or something small and local like Crystal Palace Criterium.

Track.

Track cycling is loads of fun – and it’s much more accessible than you might think.
Even if you don’t fancy racing (yet!), it’s amazing for building your fitness and skills.
Inspirational much?

Cyclocross.

Cyclocross is one of the easiest forms of cycle racing to get involved in and arguably the most fun,
especially if you’ve never done any form of competitive cycling before.
We have a large representation from Velo Cycling taking part in training and racing throughout the season.
